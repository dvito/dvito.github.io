Projects

I've worked on a bunch of projects in the past few years, here is a collection of a few.

Nepalese Election

The 2013 Nepalese elections were a full of political turmoil, and it was a desire for many to make the information on the results as available as possible to the public. DataNepal was able to provide quickly updated election results through the day utilizing data from the folks at Himal Media

Healthcare.gov Plan Finder

Plan Finder was the precursor to the modern healthcare.gov market places. I worked on the development of the front end interface that users could use to look up healthcare plan information and contact providers to obtain coverage. I also developed the API that is still used today by several companies to gain access to update information on healthcare plans.

Flu Vaccination Map

The Flu Vaccination Map was a project to make flu vaccination data available to the public in an easy to use format. It included information on demographic disparities between various public groups. Data was made available on the state, county, and zip code level. The data was available in a visual format, a table format, as well as a JSON api.
